# EIA-860 code reference (fuel types and prime movers)

Source (verify against the live form instructions, do not trust blindly): https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia860/ and the eGRID code lookup at https://www.epa.gov/sites/production/files/2017-01/egrid_code_lookup.xlsx

These are the factual EIA energy-source and prime-mover codes used on the generation side. Use them to reconcile EIA fuel codes and prime-mover codes against the plain-language descriptions on the EPA emissions side.

## Energy source (fuel) codes

- NG: Natural Gas
- BIT: Bituminous Coal
- SUB: Subbituminous Coal
- LIG: Lignite Coal
- RC: Refined Coal
- DFO: Distillate Fuel Oil
- RFO: Residual Fuel Oil
- KER: Kerosene
- JF: Jet Fuel
- PG: Gaseous Propane
- BFG: Blast Furnace Gas
- OG: Other Gas
- WDS: Wood/Wood Waste Solids
- MSW: Municipal Solid Waste
- LFG: Landfill Gas
- OBG: Other Biomass Gas
- PC: Petroleum Coke
- WC: Waste Coal

## Prime mover codes

- ST: Steam Turbine (including nuclear, geothermal and solar steam; does not include combined cycle)
- GT: Combustion (Gas) Turbine (does not include the combustion turbine part of a combined cycle; see code CT)
- CT: Combined-Cycle Combustion Turbine Part
- CA: Combined-Cycle Steam Turbine Part (also the associated heat recovery steam generator)
- CS: Combined-Cycle Single-Shaft (one combustion turbine and one steam turbine sharing a single generator)
- IC: Internal Combustion (diesel, piston, reciprocating) Engine
- CC: Combined-Cycle Total Unit (use only for plant-level aggregation)
