# Planning office context brief

After recent public scrutiny of distribution outages and fire risk, the interconnection planning office is hardening its fast-track screening. The real regulatory backdrop for these screens is public: FERC SGIP (https://www.ferc.gov/sites/default/files/2020-04/sm-gen-procedures.pdf), California Rule 21 (https://www.pge.com/tariffs/assets/pdf/tariffbook/ELEC_RULES_21.pdf), and the New York State SIR used by Con Edison (https://dps.ny.gov/system/files/documents/2026/05/24-e-0621-sirs-effective-february-9-2026.pdf). This brief and the queue are the office's own working artifacts; the tariff excerpts are verbatim from those public sources and the feeder grid figures are declared-synthetic values derived from the public screen structures for this exercise.
